About Us

At Kribuddha Wellness Center, we believe true luxury is a life free from stress, pain & imbalance. Our philosophy blends Yoga, Meditation & Mindfulness with a premium holistic touch.

A premium sanctuary of wellness, balance & strength:

Mission & Vision

Mission

To guide individuals and communities towards a pain-free, balanced, and harmonious life through yoga, meditation, and holistic wellness. We offer personalized consultations and group practices that inspire transformation in body, mind, and spirit.

Vision

To be recognized as a global sanctuary of wellness, where people experience healing, inner peace, and elevated living. Kribuddha blends ancient wisdom with modern practices for a healthier, more conscious world.

Meet Reetu Varma – Founder & Wellness Expert

Reetu Varma is a Physiotherapist, Yoga Therapist, and Wellness Coach, and the guiding force behind Kribuddha Wellness Center. With a strong foundation in physiotherapy and years of experience in holistic healing, she brings together modern medical science and ancient wellness practices to create programs that truly transform lives.

Her areas of expertise include:

  • Physiotherapy for pain management, rehabilitation, and mobility improvement
  • Yoga therapy for flexibility, strength, and inner balance
  • Holistic wellness coaching for stress relief, lifestyle improvement, and emotional well-being

“Healing is a journey, not a quick fix. My mission is to help people live pain-free, balanced, and fulfilled lives.” — Reetu Varma
